Privacy Policy

At Velo, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

Information We Collect

Velo collects only anonymous crash reports to help us improve the stability and performance of the application. These reports do not contain any personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Add-ons

Velo supports third-party add-ons to enhance your media experience. Please be aware that these add-ons are developed and maintained by third parties and may have their own privacy policies. We strongly recommend that you review the privacy policy of any add-on before installing or using it.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
