Features

tvOS First: Native tvOS experience.

Smart Playback: Tracks progress for all your content.

Content Discovery: TMDB integration, Streamio add-on (catalog and streams).

Custom Shelves: Organize movies, TV, networks, and anime, and create your own custom shelves.

Intuitive tvOS: Optimized interface, smooth playback with an internal built-in player.

Personal Library: Manage your watch list.

How-to

Add a Stremio Addon to expand your content library.

Build your own custom media shelves to organize content.

Edit existing media shelves to fine-tune your organization.

Tips

Context Menu: Long press on cards to reveal alternative options. For example, recently watched items can be deleted, and the play button can show alternative streams if more than one is available.

Remote App for URLs: Use your iPhone's Remote app to easily copy/paste long add-on URLs into Velo.

Customizable Home Screen: Home screen media shelves can be enabled or disabled in the app's settings.

Personal TMDB API Key: For full functionality, get a free personal TMDB API key and add it in the app's settings.
